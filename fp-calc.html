Fixed-Point Arithmetic Notes: • Add / Sub: Operate directly on raw integers. Watch for overflow.
• Multiply: Raw product has 2× fractional bits (Qm.n × Qm.n = Q2m.2n). Result is right-shifted by n to fit back into Qm.n.
• Divide: Dividend is left-shifted by n before integer division to preserve fractional precision.
